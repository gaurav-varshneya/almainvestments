Who Are We?

Alma Investment Group is a student run investment club founded by students at the Sauder School of Business. We follow a bottom-up, absolute returns oriented approach to investing. Our mission is to increase the value of our portfolio in the long run, as well as create a base for educating our members on security selection and business analysis.
