Who Are We?

Alma Investment Group is a student run investment club with a bottom-up, absolute return oriented approach to security selection. Founded in 2017, we are committed to both setting a foundation for educating our members as well as maximizing returns.

We do not participate in "cigar butt investing‚Äù in the sense that the companies we buy are expected to produce excellent returns on capital. However, we buy Net Current Asset Value stocks which have a event driven situation, that makes them a risk arbitrage investment. In short, we do not buy a diversified group of securities trading under Net Current Asset Value, despite their success in historically generating returns. Due to liquidity, value traps, erosion of value, fraud and other

We do not buy new issues, as advised against by value investors, due to the nature of Wall Street and the tendency for new issues to be overpriced. We try to find securities with an adequate margin of safety to reduce the amount of loss we can suffer from them.

Our investment goal is to avoid risk avoidance, we do not want to take undue speculative risks, or lose money in the long term, which is why we have taken an absolute returns approach to measuring our performance.
